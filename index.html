Welcome to Cần Thơ

Nestled in the heart of the Mekong Delta, Cần Thơ is Vietnam’s largest river city, where floating markets, lush rice fields, and vibrant culture come alive. Explore the "Western Capital" of Vietnam, taste its legendary cuisine, and cruise along the Hau River for an unforgettable adventure.
